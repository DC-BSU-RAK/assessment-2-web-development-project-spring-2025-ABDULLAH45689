Final Fantasy XVI

PS5 RPG June 22, 2023
5/5

Final Fantasy XVI delivers an epic narrative with breathtaking visuals and satisfying combat Final Fantasy 16 is an awe-inspiring masterpiece that takes the long-standing franchise to new heights. As a lifelong fan of the series, I can confidently say that this game delivers on every front, providing a captivating narrative, breathtaking visuals, and engaging gameplay that will leave players spellbound for hours on end. Without a doubt, Final Fantasy 16 deserves a solid five-star rating. First and foremost, the story of Final Fantasy 16 is a true tour de force. It weaves a complex and emotionally-driven tale filled with intricate plotlines, compelling characters, and shocking twists that kept me on the edge of my seat throughout my entire playthrough. The developers have created a world that feels alive and immersive, and I found myself genuinely invested in the struggles and triumphs of each character. The writing is exceptional, effortlessly blending moments of heart-wrenching drama with lighthearted humor, ensuring a truly memorable experience. Visually, Final Fantasy 16 is an absolute marvel. The graphics are nothing short of breathtaking, with meticulously crafted environments, stunning character designs, and spellbinding magical effects that make every scene a feast for the eyes. Whether exploring bustling cities or venturing into untamed wilderness, the level of detail is astonishing, immersing players in a world that feels alive and vibrant. The gameplay in Final Fantasy 16 is a delightful fusion of traditional RPG elements and innovative mechanics. The combat system is fast-paced, strategic, and immensely satisfying. The introduction of a unique job system adds depth and variety, allowing players to tailor their playstyle to their preferences. Whether you prefer casting spells from afar or diving into the heart of battle with a sword, Final Fantasy 16 offers an abundance of options to suit any playstyle. Furthermore, the soundtrack of Final Fantasy 16 is a triumph in itself. The melodies are enchanting and evocative, perfectly complementing the game's emotional beats and amplifying the intensity of key moments. The voice acting is superb, bringing the characters to life with stellar performances that further deepen the connection between players and the game's world. Lastly, the attention to detail and the sheer amount of content in Final Fantasy 16 is truly remarkable. From side quests and exploration to challenging boss battles and hidden secrets, there is always something new to discover. The developers have clearly poured their hearts and souls into creating a game that will keep players engaged for countless hours, ensuring a truly rewarding and unforgettable experience. In conclusion, Final Fantasy 16 is a shining gem in the crown of the franchise. It excels in every aspect, from its captivating story and stunning visuals to its engaging gameplay and exceptional sound design. Whether you're a long-time fan or a newcomer to the series, this game is an absolute must-play. Prepare to embark on an epic journey beyond imagination and lose yourself in a world of magic, intrigue, and unforgettable characters. Final Fantasy 16 truly earns its five-star rating and stands as a testament to the unparalleled brilliance of the series....

Star Wars Jedi: Survivor

PC Action May 15, 2023
4.5/5

A worthy sequel that improves upon its predecessor in nearly every way, despite some technical issuesThis was my favorite video game of 2023. It's well paced, well written, and pretty well done. It's one of my favorite additions to Star Wars and it levels up its predecessor (Jedi: Fallen Order) in ever way. Gameplay: The combat has been improved for the better. Lot's of combat in the story and even more during exploration. You can easily change the difficulty at anytime throughout the game baed on the experience that you want. There are new force abilities and new lightsaber tricks that truly reflect how Cal has improved between the two games. Only issue you may find with combat is the camera, especially in closed spaces. The narrative is a worthy continuation of Cal's story and it's got perfect moment throughout the story that constantly keep you interested in the game. The exploration has definitely been improved from Fallen Order, with a big new world (Koboh) that has plenty to offer the player in exploration. There are other worlds to explore as well including the well known Capital of the Galactic Empire, Coruscant. Lots of chests to open which give you plenty of items for customization. Customization has definitely improved from Fallen Order. You can customize every element of Cal's lightsaber and have plenty of things to choose from (if you find all the chests, that is). You can also customize BD-1 with more detail and color and you can customize almost everything about Cal, from his hair down to his shoes. I just wanted to throw this in here because I am a big fan of music in film and video games, but THE SCORE! This game has some of the best music I've ever heard, especially in Star Wars. I have to point out that Stephen Barton and Gordy Haab won a Grammy for their work on this game and it was so well deserved. I give it five stars. I had one of the best video gaming experiences I ever had playing this thing and it's my favorite story from a Star Wars spinoff. I'm so excited for the next Jedi game....
